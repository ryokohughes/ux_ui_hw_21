Who is Danbo?

Origin

Danbo, or Danboard, is a character from the manga "Yotsuba&!".

The cardboard box robot has inspired countless memes, spin-offs and items of merchandise. So perhaps it was only natural that he eventually became his own robot.

Merchandising

Danbo was later picked up as an Internet meme, and inspired various electronic gadgets.
